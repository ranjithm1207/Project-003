Privacy Guarantees
How differential privacy protects patient data

🏥 Data Never Leaves

Raw patient records stay at each hospital. Only model gradients (mathematical abstractions) are transmitted to the central server.

🎲 Noise Injection

Calibrated Gaussian noise is added to model updates before aggregation, providing (ε,δ)-differential privacy guarantees.

✂️ Gradient Clipping

Per-sample gradient clipping bounds the influence of any single patient record, preventing memorization attacks.
